A Curious Mind's Evolution

My academic journey began with an insatiable curiosity about the world around me. Initially drawn to the fundamental sciences, I explored chemistry and electrical engineering, fascinated by how things work at their most basic level. However, it was during these explorations that I discovered something even more compelling: the power of data to reveal hidden patterns and insights.
